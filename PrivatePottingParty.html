Private Potting Parties

Invite your friends to a Potting Party!

Bring your friends to a private potting party at Heather Hill Gardens. Just call to reserve your space and leave the rest to us! Relax, have fun, and plant some pots with your friends.

We make it easy. You pick the plants, and we'll provide potting soil and fertilizer. And we'll help you design your containers for your taste and location. Bring your own containers or browse our hand-selected collection of containers and saucers.

This is a great activity for book clubs, quilting bees, family groups, any time you want some time to socialize with a small group.
