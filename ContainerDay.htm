We make it easy. You pick from our collection of plants specially selected to thrive in Northern Virginia, and we'll provide potting soil and fertilizer. And we'll help you design your containers for your taste and location.

Bring your gloves, your pots, and your friends. It'll be fun!

 

 

Garden Tip

Group containers together for a lush, green look. Using a variety of plant and container heights creates additional interest. Bonus? It's easier to water containers that are gathered in a few central locations.
