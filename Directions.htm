Directions

Heather Hill Gardens is located at 8111 Ox Road (Rt. 123) in Fairfax Station, Virginia.

From the South

  • From Northbound I-95, take exit 160B, Rt. 123 North
  • Continue north on 123, staying in the right lanes, approximately 5 miles
  • Heather Hill Gardens is on the right 1/10 mile past the traffic light at Silverbrook Road. Look for the blue Victorian house.
