Garden Tips

Start your herbs early so that you can enjoy the fresh taste of summer all season long.

 

Try fresh basil on your grocery store pizza. Mmm.

 

Rosemary stems make excellent skewers for grilling meat or veggies. Soak them first so that they survive the heat.

 

Group your herbs together in a large container to conserve water. Large containers retain water better and so you can wait longer between waterings.
