Plants and Produce!
We've always been known for our wide variety of beautiful plants. But did you know we also have produce? Visit us for fresh, healthy food for your family. We regularly have tomatoes, corn, cucumbers, zucchini, cantaloupe, and peaches. Come visit and see what other yummy treats we have.

It's not too late to add some lovely color and structure to your gardens and containers. Try a hosta in a pot with some colorful annuals for a lovely feature. Or consider an unexpected vessel for planting; it may turn out to be your favorite!
